What is this platform about?

This platform centralizes grades, announcements, and course information in one place, making it easier for students and instructors to stay organized and informed.

Problems that it solves

Scattered academic information

Students often miss grades or announcements because information is shared across emails, messaging apps, and notice boards. This platform solves this by centralizing everything in one place.

Delayed or unclear communication

Important updates are sometimes sent late or without clear context. This platform provides timely, organized announcements that everyone can access easily.

Inefficient grade publishing

Instructors rely on manual or informal methods to share results, which can cause errors and confusion. This platform allows instructors to publish grades securely and consistently in one system.
