About Us

We are a team of dedicated students working together to create a smart and user-friendly Student Portal. Each of us brings unique skills in design, development, and problem-solving. Our goal is to build a platform that makes campus life easier, more organized, and more connected for every student.

Key Features

Course Management

Students can view and manage their courses, from Biology to Computer Science, all within the portal. The easy-to-navigate dashboard allows for quick access to course content and assignments.

Gradebook Access

Students can instantly view their grades and performance metrics, helping them to identify areas for improvement and celebrate their successes.

Anouncement Access

Students can instantly view the new anouncements and class related newses helping the to get information early
